Our coffee shop was established in 2005 with the desire to bring the full delicious flavor of Dak Nong coffee beans to our dear customers.

We not only focus on the quality of coffee but also create a cozy and friendly space where customers can relax and chat. Coming to the shop, you will enjoy wonderful moments with friends and family.

In addition to coffee, we also serve a variety of delicious drinks and pastries to satisfy our customers' needs. Come and experience our wonderful coffee space, where taste and dedication always come first.
